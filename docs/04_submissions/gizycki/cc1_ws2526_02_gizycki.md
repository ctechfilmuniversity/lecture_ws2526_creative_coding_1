### Task 02.03 - _Algorithmic Thinking_

>Briefly reflect on how you implemented your creative idea. Did you apply algorithmic thinking and if so, how?

>_Submission_: Text or bullet points in your markdown submission file.

- I translated my idea of happiness into simple visual rules.
- The mouse position controls the position of a sun.
- A loop is used to draw evenly distributed sun rays around the sun.
- Each mouse click creates a heart at the current position.
- The hearts are stored in a list and redrawn every frame.
- This structured approach allowed me to build the visual step by step using algorithmic thinking.

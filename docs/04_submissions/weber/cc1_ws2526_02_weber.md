## Task 02.03 - Algorithmic Thinking

In both sketches, I implemented my ideas by defining simple rules for position, growth, and movement, which are repeatedly applied over time. Algorithmic thinking is used here by structuring the behavior of many elements through loops or conditions and time-based changes instead of manually placing or animating each element. I tried to find approaches that are/seem "random" but create an overall coherent picture.
